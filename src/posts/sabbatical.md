# Sabbatical

<img src="{{ '/images/Nyhavn.JPG' | url }}" alt="Friends and family at Nyhavn in Copenhagen" class="post-featured-image" />

April was an incredible month! I enjoyed a month-long sabbatical (a 10-year anniversary gift from Lumen that I used a year late). It was a fantastic, much-appreciated gift, and made for a wonderful break. The family and I headed to Denmark for a week to reunite with dear friends. They showed us around Copenhagen and Aarhus, and we fell in love with the Scandinavian way of life. Then, we visited another friend in Germany and made a quick stop in Bremen before arriving in Amsterdam. My brother recently started a new job there, so we got to visit with him and his family. We arrived while the blossoms were blooming, so we took a trip out to the tulip fields and just enjoyed our time together. It was really magical. Upon returning home, I got sick, so I spent a week or so moping around the house. I had just enough energy to slowly work on an intensive spring cleaning purge, and then spent the rest of my spare time working on a fun side project—an improv website.
