# Authoring Assessments

<img src="{{ '/images/projects/authoring-assessments.png' | url }}" alt="Authoring Assessments tutorial" class="post-featured-image" />

<div class="problem-solution">
  <div class="problem-solution-col">
    <div class="section-eyebrow">The Problem</div>
    <p>Subject matter experts had no consistent training for writing effective assessments, leading to uneven quality across courses.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="problem-solution-col">
    <div class="section-eyebrow">The Solution</div>
    <p>An H5P tutorial walking SMEs through Lumen's assessment authoring process, used as a training standard for several years.</p>
  </div>
</div>

This [tutorial](https://lumenlearning.h5p.com/content/1291195720097981998) was created for the purpose of training subject matter experts to author assessments for Lumen Learning. This system is no longer used at Lumen, but this was a valuable resource in training subject matter experts for several years.
