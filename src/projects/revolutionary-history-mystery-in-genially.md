# Revolutionary History Mystery in Genially

<img src="{{ '/images/projects/revolutionary-history-mystery.png' | url }}" alt="Revolutionary History Mystery in Genially" class="post-featured-image" />

For this project in Designing Learning Systems with Simulation and Game Technology, I worked with my classmate Brenda Bradick to create a learning game designed for US History students to use when reviewing concepts connected to the Revolutionary War. We had great ideas for this game, and had originally hoped to create a much more complex version with a large deck of cards that would be played similar to the game "Chronology," but we found our time and software constraints forced us in a different direction. We chose to work inside of Genially and were pleased with its simplicity and design. It's a great resource for building out branching activities.

[Play the Revolutionary History Mystery game here](https://view.genial.ly/617b06332af7f50d70bc3c46/interactive-content-history-breakout)
