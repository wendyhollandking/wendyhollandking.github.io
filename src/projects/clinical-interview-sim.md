# Clinical Interview Simulation

<img src="{{ '/images/projects/clinical-interview-sim.png' | url }}" alt="Clinical Interview Simulation" class="post-featured-image" />

I've been meeting with a psychologist and AI-expert, Joe Slade, about ways to help students get more familiar with AI and more comfortable using AI for use cases outside of just as a "cheating-helper" or "quiz-taker." Joe has some phenomenal ideas that have sparked a lot of great conversations and food for thought. One idea he had was to program AI to respond as a patient in order to give students practice with the diagnostic process. I thought it'd be fun to mock this up in a more structured way, so I played with Claude Code to create this diagnostic activity.
